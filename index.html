Sweet Chocolate Facts

World's Largest Bar

The world's largest chocolate bar was created in the UK and weighed over 5.7 tonnes, or roughly the same as an adult African elephant.

Ancient Currency

Before it was a delicious treat, cacao beans were so valuable that ancient Mesoamerican civilizations, including the Aztecs, used them as a form of currency.

Toxicity to Dogs

Chocolate contains a compound called theobromine, which is harmless to humans but can be toxic to dogs, even in small amounts.

A Lot of Beans

It takes the harvest from a full year's growth of one cacao tree, approximately 400 cacao beans, to produce just one pound of chocolate.
